Our Story

Modern Organic Living
Inspired by Traditional Wisdom

What started as a passion for preserving traditional food wisdom became Trivenik Organics Private Limited in 2020.

The Beginning

From Tradition to Table

Trivenik Lakadi Ghana was born from a simple, deeply personal frustration: the foods that sustained Indian families for millennia had all but disappeared from modern kitchens. Wood-pressed oils replaced by chemically refined ones. Stone-ground flours swapped for bleached, stripped alternatives. Ancient millets forgotten in favour of refined grains that cause inflammation and sugar spikes.

Our founders — raised in households where the ghani's rhythmic grinding was a morning soundtrack and freshly pressed oil filled the air with warmth — decided this didn't have to be the future for every Indian family.

The Mission

Our Purpose

Our mission is to help families return to healthier eating habits through authentic and minimally processed foods. We believe that real nutrition doesn't need to be invented — it needs to be remembered.

Incorporated as Trivenik Organics Private Limited in 2020 and based in Pune, Maharashtra, we started with a small wood-pressed oil facility and have since grown to offer a full ecosystem of traditional nutrition products.

From Tradition to Technology

While our values come from tradition, our manufacturing follows modern standards of hygiene, consistency, and quality assurance. We combine traditional food wisdom with modern manufacturing practices, rigorous quality control systems, and customer-centric processes — to create products you can trust unconditionally.
